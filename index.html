Why is Trappist-1 so awesome?

Trappist-1 is a nearby ultra-cool red dwarf star, located about 40 light-years away from our Sun. The star system has 7 rocky planets - all of them with the potential of water on their surface, some far more than our Earth. Also, all of the 7 planets orbit inside of the habitable zone, not too close but not too far from the star.
